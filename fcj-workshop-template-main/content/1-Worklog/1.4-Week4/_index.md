### Week 4 Objectives:

* Understand and practice the use of AWS Snow Family, Storage Gateway, and Backup services for hybrid cloud environments.
* Learn about various AWS service categories including Compute, Storage, Networking, and Database.
* Gain hands-on experience in importing and exporting virtual machines (VMs) to and from AWS.
* Learn to mount and manage file shares on on-premises systems using Amazon FSx and EFS.
* Explore techniques to monitor and optimize storage performance, including deduplication, shadow copies, and throughput scaling.
